HOW TO STAY SAFE FROM COVID-19

~ Veer

Wash Your Hands Regularly

Regular hand wash with soap or sanitizer for 30 secs will help you avoid germs

Cover Your Mouth And Nose

Cover your mouth and nose while sneezing or coughing in public. Even better use a mask

Stay Home Stay Safe

Avoid travelling or being in crowded places. Preferrably stay indoors

Eat Healthy

Avoid eating junk food. Eat as healthy as possible to boost immunity.

Stay Calm

Regular excersice and meditation is a way to keep yourself calm and composed.

BE AWARE. BE SAFE.
